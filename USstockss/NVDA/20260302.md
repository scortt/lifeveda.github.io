# NVDA 周日线跟踪：关键缺口支撑与 170 破位风险（2026-03-02） | NVDA Weekly-Daily Watch: Key Gap Support and 170 Breakdown Risk (2026-03-02)

## 周线观察 | Weekly Read

周线上有明显缺口：`2024` 年高点在 `152.65`，`2025` 年低点在 `164.17`，而 `164.17` 也是一个重要多头支撑参考。  
A clear weekly gap is visible: the `2024` high is `152.65` and the `2025` low is `164.17`, with `164.17` also acting as an important bull support reference.

近期卖方压力确实偏强，且价格已经跌破 `EMA20`。  
Recent selling pressure has been notable, and price has already fallen below `EMA20`.

相对积极的一点是，目前还没有形成典型的牛市 `EMA20` 缺口形态，因此周线大级别上还不能直接判定为彻底转空，但结构已明显脆弱。  
A modest positive point is that a classic bull-market `EMA20` gap pattern has not fully formed yet, so the higher-timeframe weekly context is not conclusively bearish, though it is clearly fragile.

## 日线观察 | Daily Read

日线上升趋势早已结束，目前更像长期交易区间。  
The daily uptrend has ended for a while, and the structure now resembles an extended trading range.

若有效跌破 `170` 一线，日线将更明确转入空头趋势。  
If price breaks decisively below `170`, the daily chart would more clearly transition into a bear trend.

当前交易假设可先定义为“日线 `TR`，观察是否向下突破”。  
For now, the practical thesis is "daily `TR`, then monitor for downside breakout."
