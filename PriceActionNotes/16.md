# 价格行为学视频笔记 16：宽通道、突破概率与逆势 Scalp | Price Action Video Notes 16: Broad Channels, Breakout Odds, and Countertrend Scalps

## 宽通道中的突破概率 | Breakout Odds in Broad Channels

在宽通道里，顺原方向继续突破的概率通常只有约 `25%`。  
In a broad channel, continuation breakout in the original direction is often only around `25%`.

这意味着越到后段，向相反方向突破的概率会提高到约 `75%`。  
This means that later in the channel, the chance of a breakout in the opposite direction can rise toward `75%`.

## 顺势与逆势 Scalp 的阶段切换 | Switching from With-Trend to Countertrend Scalps

宽通道早段更适合顺势 `Scalp`，后段应逐步切换到逆势 `Scalp`。  
In early broad-channel phase, with-trend `Scalp` is often better; later phase favors countertrend `Scalp`.

这种切换核心是跟随概率变化，而不是坚持单一交易方向。  
The key is adapting to probability shifts instead of forcing one directional bias.

## 突破后的执行 | Post-Breakout Execution

一旦有效突破出现，执行上要考虑持续买入或持续跟随，而不是只做一次入场。  
Once a valid breakout appears, execution should consider sustained buying or continuation participation instead of a one-shot entry.

## 后期大趋势 K 线与缺口性质 | Late-Stage Large Trend Bars and Gap Type

宽通道运行很久后，如果出现顺势大 K 线，它更可能是衰竭缺口，而不是测量缺口。  
After a long broad-channel run, a large trend bar in trend direction is more likely an exhaustion gap than a measuring gap.

虽然有例外，但实战上先按“衰竭优先”处理会更稳健。  
Exceptions exist, but treating it as exhaustion first is often more robust in practice.

## 反转后的常见路径 | Typical Path After Reversal

衰竭后的反转不一定直接变成反趋势，很多时候先进入 `Trading Range`。  
A reversal after exhaustion does not always become an opposite trend immediately; it often first becomes a `Trading Range`.

随后才可能从区间再演化为明确反趋势。  
Only later may that range evolve into a clear opposite trend.

## 通道线测试：Overshoot 更常见 | Channel-Line Tests: Overshoots Are More Common

通道边缘测试里，大多数是 `Overshoot`，`Undershoot` 相对少见。  
In channel-line tests, `Overshoot` is common, while `Undershoot` is relatively rare.

因此看到价格冲出通道边缘时，要警惕陷阱与衰竭。  
So when price pushes beyond channel boundaries, be alert for traps and exhaustion.

## 区间与通道的识别困难 | Difficulty Distinguishing Trading Range vs Channel

有些阶段很难清楚区分 `Trading Range` 和 `Channel`。  
At times it is difficult to cleanly distinguish a `Trading Range` from a `Channel`.

无论归类为何，执行上都可回到同一原则：`Buy Low, Sell High, Scalp`。  
Regardless of label, execution can return to the same principle: `Buy Low, Sell High, Scalp`.

## 第二信号与双向通道认知 | Second Signal and Two-Sided Channel Reality

尤其在第二个信号出现时，要更重视反向机会与风控。  
Especially at the second signal, give more weight to opposite-direction opportunities and risk control.

每个交易区间里通常都会同时存在牛通道和熊通道。  
Every trading range typically contains both a bull channel and a bear channel.

这种双向并存是正常现象，不代表分析错误。  
This two-sided coexistence is normal and does not imply analysis failure.
