# 价格行为学视频笔记 13：持续持仓与始终在场 | Price Action Video Notes 13: Holding Through Trends and Always-In Direction

## 趋势与区间 | Trend vs Trading Range

市场要不然在趋势当中，要不然在交易区间当中。  
The market is either in a trend or in a trading range.

交易区间最典型的特征就是困惑感。  
Confusion is a hallmark of a trading range.

## 始终在场方向 | Always-In Direction

如果趋势已经形成，优先考虑挂突破单顺势入场。即使止损略大，确定性通常更好，尤其是在第一个回调之前。  
If a trend is already underway, prefer stop-entry orders in trend direction. Even with a wider stop, certainty is often better, especially before the first pullback.

执行错误时不要慌，继续根据 K 线信息动态判断，并寻找更优的撤退机会。  
If execution is wrong, do not panic; keep reassessing bar-by-bar and look for cleaner exit opportunities.

如果没有出现反向趋势信号，就不急于离场，优先延续原趋势方向。  
If there is no clear opposite-trend signal, avoid exiting too early and prioritize continuation in the original direction.

在 `Always In` 方向上交易，通常更容易稳定赚钱。  
It is often easier to make money trading in the `Always In` direction.

## 止盈与再入场 | Taking Profit and Re-entry

`Always In` 的思路不等于永不止盈。止盈之后，要继续寻找同方向的新入场机会。  
`Always In` does not mean never taking profit. After profit-taking, continue searching for new entries in the same direction.

这里的“始终在场”更准确地说是“始终寻找顺方向机会”，不是机械地一直持有同一仓位。  
“Always in” is better understood as “always looking for opportunities in the dominant direction,” not mechanically holding the same position forever.

## 止损管理 | Stop Placement

保护性止损可以放在强势突破 K 线的下方（多头）或上方（空头），因为强势突破理论上不应回破该位置。  
A protective stop can be placed below (for bulls) or above (for bears) the strong breakout bar, because a strong breakout should not quickly fail through that level.

如果采用这种止损方式，入场前就应明确止盈计划，例如 `1R` 或 `2R`。  
If you use this stop model, define the take-profit framework in advance, such as `1R` or `2R`.

## 回调买入规则 | Pullback-Entry Rule

在回调中买入时，止损更适合放在前一腿结构位置，而不是仅放在本次回调低点。  
When buying a pullback, stops are often better placed at the prior leg structure, not only at the current pullback low.

止盈可先按 `1:1` 盈亏比执行；只有当上行突破已被明确确认后，再考虑把止损上移到回调位置。  
A `1:1` risk-reward take-profit is a practical baseline; only after a clear upside breakout confirmation should you consider moving the stop up to the pullback zone.

## 形态筛选 | Pattern Selection

重点寻找“非比寻常”的强势形态，并沿该方向做延续交易。  
Focus on unusually strong patterns, then trade continuation in that same direction.
