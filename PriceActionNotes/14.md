# 价格行为学视频笔记 14：趋势与通道 | Price Action Video Notes 14: Trends and Channels

## 趋势定义 | Trend Definition

趋势意味着价格大部分时间朝一个方向运行。  
A trend means prices move mostly in one direction.

趋势通常被通道包裹，价格在趋势线与通道线之间推进。  
A trend is often contained in a channel, with price advancing between trend and channel lines.

趋势可以持续任意数量的 K 线，没有固定长度。  
A trend can last for any number of bars; there is no fixed bar count.

在一张图里，主趋势通常只有 `1` 到 `2` 段最清晰。  
On one chart, only `1` to `2` main trend legs are usually the clearest.

## 牛市结构与止损 | Bull Structure and Stops

牛市最典型的特征是更高的低点（HL）不断出现。  
A classic bull trend feature is a sequence of higher lows (HLs).

下方用趋势线连接低点，上方用通道线衡量推进斜率。  
Use a trend line below to connect lows, and a channel line above to measure momentum.

在趋势里，合理止损通常会比较远。  
In a trend, logical stops are often relatively far.

无论做波段还是 `Scalp`，止损都应放在主要支撑/阻力之外：牛市放在关键低点下方，熊市放在关键高点上方。  
Whether trading swings or `Scalp`, stops should be beyond key support/resistance: below major lows in bull trends and above major highs in bear trends.

## 宽通道特征 | Broad Bull Channel

牛市宽通道常见“更高高点 + 更高低点”，但回调会更深。  
A broad bull channel often still has higher highs and higher lows, but pullbacks are deeper.

这类结构里，执行上要接受更大波动，不要把深回调误判成趋势结束。  
In this structure, execution must tolerate larger swings and avoid mistaking deep pullbacks for immediate trend termination.

## 动态画线 | Dynamic Line Work

要持续画线并根据新价格行为更新线条。  
Keep drawing lines continuously and update them as new price action appears.

当结构发生变化时，及时重画趋势线与通道线，避免固守旧判断。  
When structure changes, redraw trend and channel lines promptly instead of anchoring to outdated assumptions.

## 突破、通道与交易选择 | Breakout, Channel, and Trade Selection

`Breakout` 往往代表更强趋势动能，而 `Channel` 常见于趋势减速阶段。  
`Breakout` often represents stronger trend momentum, while `Channel` usually appears when trend speed is slowing.

跳空开盘叠加 `1` 根或多根强趋势 K 线，通常说明方向性更强。  
A gap open plus `1` or more strong trend bars usually signals stronger directional intent.

有些 `Breakout` 不是单根大 K 线，也可能是一串连续小实体 K 线，但结构同样有效。  
Some `Breakout` phases are not one large bar; they can be a sequence of smaller strong bars and still be valid.

趋势形态不同，交易方式也要随之调整。  
Different trend structures require different execution styles.

总体上，趋势环境优先 `Swing Trade`，不宜只做机械 `Scalp`。  
In general, trend environments favor `Swing Trade` over purely mechanical `Scalp` behavior.

## Spike and Channel 与陷阱管理 | Spike and Channel and Trap Management

大多数趋势都可以拆解为 `Spike + Channel` 组合。  
Most trends can be decomposed into a `Spike + Channel` sequence.

一旦判断为牛市，做空需要更谨慎，避免落入逆势做空陷阱。  
Once the market is judged bullish, shorting should be much more selective to avoid counter-trend traps.

另一个常见陷阱是：只看到风险，却忽略了概率和结构优势。  
Another common trap is focusing only on risk while ignoring probability and structural edge.

## 入场细节补充 | Entry Detail Addendum

强牛趋势里，经常没有“完美形态”的买点，持续关注 `H1` / `H2` 往往更实用。  
In strong bull trends, textbook-perfect entries are rare; tracking `H1` / `H2` continuation entries is often more practical.

在回调或震荡中，若 `H2` 位置更低但趋势仍在，反而可能提供更好的风险回报。  
In pullbacks or ranges, a lower `H2` within an intact trend can sometimes offer better risk-reward.

下跌通道常常是潜在牛旗，可能向上突破；上升通道也可能是潜在熊旗，存在向下突破概率。  
A bear channel can be a potential bull flag with upside breakout risk; a bull channel can also be a potential bear flag with downside breakout risk.
