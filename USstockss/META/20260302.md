# META 结构跟踪：月线仍强，周线进入 BOM 风险区（2026-03-02） | META Structure Watch: Monthly Strength, Weekly BOM Risk (2026-03-02)

## 月线观察 | Monthly Read

月线仍在此前的窄上升趋势里，虽然有演化为宽通道的可能，但目前价格依旧在 `EMA20` 上方。  
The monthly chart remains inside its prior tight bull trend; although it may evolve into a broader channel, price is still above `EMA20` for now.

## 周线观察 | Weekly Read

周线更值得警惕，当前更像进入 `BOM`（Breakout Mode）阶段。  
The weekly chart deserves more caution and looks closer to `BOM` (Breakout Mode).

如果在当前位置进入 `BOM`，我倾向于认为向下突破概率高于向上突破。  
If `BOM` is forming at this level, downside breakout probability appears higher than upside breakout probability.
