# 20260301 市场短评 | 20260301 Market Hottake

今天的结论很简单：在宽通道与交易区间切换阶段，先做风险控制，再谈扩张收益。  
Today's conclusion is simple: when price shifts between a broad channel and a trading range, control risk first, then think about expansion.

- 交易区间内优先“低买高卖 + 留半仓”，不要在区间中部追单。  
- In a trading range, prioritize "buy low, sell high + keep half position"; avoid chasing in the middle of the range.

- 对突破只做两类处理：有效跟随，或失败后反手；不做模糊预判。  
- Handle breakouts in only two ways: follow confirmed continuation, or fade failed breaks; avoid vague prediction.

- 若日线级别仍未脱离拥挤区，控制仓位与频率比找“完美点位”更重要。  
- If the daily timeframe is still congested, position sizing and trade frequency matter more than finding a "perfect" entry.
