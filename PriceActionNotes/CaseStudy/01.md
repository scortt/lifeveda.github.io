## 场景描述 | Setup Description

在交易区间边缘，最右侧那根 K 线看起来像突破，但其实更接近“未确认突破”的灰色地带。  
At the edge of a trading range, the rightmost bar looked like a breakout, but it was closer to an unconfirmed breakout zone.

如果在这种位置直接按突破下单，通常会面临止损过远、盈亏比恶化的问题。  
Entering immediately as a breakout here often leads to a wide stop and poor risk-reward.

## 执行反思 | Execution Reflection

更好的做法是等待下一根出现明确跟随的 K 线再入场。  
A better approach is to wait for the next bar to provide clear follow-through before entry.

虽然入场价格可能不如抢先单，但确认后的结构更清晰，止损也更容易放在合理位置。  
The entry price may look worse than an early entry, but confirmed structure is cleaner and stop placement is usually more logical.

这类错误不仅出现在交易区间，也常见于宽通道边缘。  
This mistake appears not only in trading ranges, but also near the edges of broad channels.

## 关键结论 | Key Takeaway

不要为了“看起来快要突破”而提前下注；先要跟随，再要位置。  
Do not bet early on “almost breakout” setups; prioritize follow-through first, then entry location.
